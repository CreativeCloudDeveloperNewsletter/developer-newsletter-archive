  Submission Reviews during COVID-19  
 

Please note that submission reviews for Adobe Exchange products and Adobe XD plugins may see a slight slowdown in the near future as we transition our Integrations Review team to at-home work environments. Thus far, turnaround-time for reviews is normal. We will do our best to communicate if we start to see a slowdown.
 
What you can do to prevent delays:
  • Carefully review our submission guidelines for Adobe Exchange or Adobe XD.
  • In particular, note the branding and marketing policies mentioned in the respective guidelines.
  • If your product offers service login, licensing, or any other other form of credential checking, be sure to provide test credentials along with your submission.
  • Most importantly, and as always, submit early; avoid waiting until just before a big launch or update to submit your product for review.
Another helpful resource to check out is "Seven Ways to Make Sure Your XD Plugin is Approved for the Plugin Manager", a blog article by Akshay Abrol from the CC Integrations Review team. While focused on XD plugins, it contains useful advice for anyone submitting a product for review.
 
We'll do our best to communicate review status if we start to see meaningful delays.
