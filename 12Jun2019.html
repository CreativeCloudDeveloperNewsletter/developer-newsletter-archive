XD API News
XD 20 is out now, and big updates are on the way in XD 21, including an under-the-hood transition to UXP 3. The transition should be seamless and most XD plugins won’t experience unexpected changes.

In some cases, there could be differences in how your UI is rendered. For the few plugins that could be affected, we’ve already reached out to you directly to give you an early heads up. But even if you haven’t heard from us, it’s a good idea to check before XD 21 is released.
