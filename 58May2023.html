InDesign 18.3 C++ Plugin Change Deferred
The InDesign team has deferred the Boost upgrade we announced last month to a subsequent release of InDesign. A recompile of C++ plug-ins will not be required for InDesign 18.3. For more information, read the InDesign Developers Prerelease.
