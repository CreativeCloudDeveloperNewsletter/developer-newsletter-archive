Keep your listing in the EU after Feb 16th
To keep your listing(s) available to EU customers without interruption, submit your "trader information" by February 16, 2025. This applies to public add-ons in the Adobe Express add-on marketplace and any kind of listing on Adobe Exchange (for example, Plugins, Scripts, Panels, Extensions, Brushes, Templates, Presets, Filters, LUTs, Overlays, Actions, Backgrounds, etc.).

New capabilities for Adobe Express
importPdf() and importPresentation() APIs can now be used in add-ons published to the add-on marketplace. The experimentalApis flag is no longer required to utilize these APIs. We've also added a new API to help you bring elements into view so you can focus the user's attention on a specific page. For those using addAnimatedImage, you can now add GIFs with resolutions up to 1080px.
