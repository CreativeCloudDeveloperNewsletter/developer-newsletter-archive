Photoshop Manifest v. 5 and the Marketplace
Manifest v5 is here. Check out the documentation or read our announcement on the blog. Here are some important notes on moving to manifest v. 5, in regards to updating your Exchange Marketplace listing:
  • You may still submit v5 manifests to the Exchange, as long as the Photoshop minVersion in your manifest is 23.3.0.
  • For your plugin to support Photoshop 23.2.x and below, you should submit v4 manifests.
  • Note that v4 manifests do continue to work in Photoshop 23.3.0, but you won’t be able to benefit from the new features enabled in v5 (see the blog post for details).
  • If you update your existing plugin to manifest v5, users who have not previously installed your plugin must upgrade to Photoshop 23.3.0 or higher in order to install the current version of the plugin. (The marketplace will not attempt to deliver an older version of the plugin.)

Please join the Content Authenticity Initiative (CAI) team for an in-depth workshop exploring Adobe’s three open-source products with easy-to-implement toolkits for digital content provenance.
