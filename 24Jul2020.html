  Adobe Fund for Design to Commission Development Projects  
 

The Adobe Fund for Design just announced two important updates. Adobe will be expanding the scope of the Adobe Fund for Design to commission certain development projects, including UXP plugins. This is intended to provide more opportunities for financial support for members of our developer and partner communities being affected by the economic effects of Covid-19.
 
Going forward, the fund aims to deploy at least 50% of its resources to teams and individuals from underrepresented communities – be it by race/ethnicity, sexual orientation, gender identity, socioeconomic background, disability, or otherwise. This is to do more to foster diversity, inclusivity, and equality within the communites we serve.
 
We encourage community members to review the list of commission projects and apply to work on a specific project. As always, individuals and teams may apply for the Adobe Fund for Design with ideas of their own.

  XD 31 Updates: Enforcement of Plugin Folder Rules  
 

XD 31 now enforces the rule that no plugins should be side-loaded manually into the plugins folder. Only plugins installed by double-clicking on a .XDX plugin package or via the XD Plugin Manager will be loaded from the plugins folder.
 
The develop folder continues to allow side-loading of plugins you are currently building, as in previous versions of XD.
