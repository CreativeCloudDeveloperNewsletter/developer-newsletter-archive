Recompile Your C++ Plug-ins for InDesign 18.3
Due to updating internal platform components, InDesign version 18.3 has a breaking change for C++ plug-ins that use Boost libraries. InDesign has updated Boost from 1.72 to 1.74. Your plug-in may need to be recompiled with the latest SDK of InDesign 18.3. For changes across Boost versions, please refer the following Boost release notes:
  • https://www.boost.org/users/history/version_1_73_0.html
  • https://www.boost.org/users/history/version_1_74_0.html
