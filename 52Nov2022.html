Introducing the Adobe Express Embed SDK
Introducing the Adobe Express Embed SDK. This brand new SDK includes both an Adobe Express Editor as well as Quick Actions, powered by Photoshop and Premiere Pro that simplify common, complex tasks. This modular, flexible SDK allows developers to choose the capabilities that best meet their users’ needs and is now in open beta.
 
We also announced at Adobe MAX that we will be partnering with Wix to explore opportunities to bring the best of Adobe Express and the Wix platform to both user bases. See the announcement.

InDesign Scripting Powered by UXP is Now Available in InDesign v. 18

Use modern JavaScript to automate tasks, customize InDesign to match your work habits and layouts or achieve effects that would be difficult or impossible by other means. InDesign Scripting Powered by UXP is available with InDesign v. 18.0, and InDesign Server v. 18.0. Check our new documentation site.
