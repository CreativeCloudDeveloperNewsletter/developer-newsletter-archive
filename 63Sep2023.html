    Bring Adobe Express to Your Users
    Adobe Express Embed SDK lets you bring Adobe Express editing capabilities into your own web application. Adobe Express is the AI-first, an all-in-one creativity app that makes it fast, easy and fun for any user to design and share standout content. The SDK lets you: 
    • Embed the Adobe Express editor so users can quickly and easily create and edit content like social media graphics, flyers, and ads using Adobe Firefly capabilities such as text-to-image and text effects alongside thousands of creative templates and design assets. 
    • Embed image and video editing capabilities with quick actions like Remove Background, Crop Image, and Merge Videos -- all powered by Adobe Express.
    Try it yourself in our demo application, or in Google Chrome using this extension.
