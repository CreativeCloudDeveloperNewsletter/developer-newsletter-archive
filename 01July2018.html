Hello!
 

Welcome to the new monthly newsletter for Creative Cloud developers. The growing Creative Cloud Developer Ecosystem team is excited to keep you up to date on important announcements, resources, and “how to” information to make it easier to build, distribute and promote on our platform.

It was great to see many of you at the Creative Developers Summit in New Orleans and our EU Partner Day in Hamburg in June to share product roadmaps, the future of extensibility and to receive feedback from you. We hope this newsletter will help us continue the dialogue.

The team has been hard at work throughout the first half of 2018. In case you haven’t seen our new weekly blog posts on the Adobe Tech Blog, here are some highlights:

  • Sign Up Now to Start Building XD plugins
    Get the inside track on Adobe XD extensibility. We’ll be reaching out soon to get developers involved early. After you sign up, read our FAQ. 
  • Adobe Announces a $10 Million Fund for Design
    We have $10 million for developers who want to have a positive impact on the future of design. Find out more or apply here.
  • New CEP Resources on GitHub
    The CC Developer Experience team has been working hard to improve the onboarding experience for CEP. Check out our new Github repo of Getting Started Guides.

Have you heard? You now get 90% revenue share on Adobe Exchange. Make sure to update your listing on the new Exchange Producer Portal. Check out this post to learn how.
 

 

Thank You!
