Exchange Partner Portal Migrated to New Developer Distribution Portal

As of June 23rd, 2023, the Exchange Partner Portal was migrated to the new Developer Distribution portal. In the new portal, you can review plugin acquisition reports, make changes to your listings without submitting a patch, and retract, recall, mark as pending and modify listings without contacting the review team. Read more about these updates in our previous blog post.

Our team is still responding to bugs and completing the migration this month. Be sure to read our updated FAQ. For any bugs or concerns, please reach out to the review team.
